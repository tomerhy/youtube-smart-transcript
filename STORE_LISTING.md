## Extension Name
YouTube Smart Transcript

## Short Description (132 characters max)
Extract, view, and export YouTube video transcripts. Copy to clipboard or download as TXT, SRT, or Markdown.

## Detailed Description

**YouTube Smart Transcript** makes it easy to extract and export transcripts from any YouTube video with captions.

**KEY FEATURES**

**Transcript Extraction**
- Automatically extracts transcripts from YouTube videos
- Supports multiple languages (manual and auto-generated captions)
- Smart paragraph grouping for easier reading

**Clickable Timestamps**
- Click any timestamp to jump directly to that moment in the video
- Perfect for navigating long videos or finding specific quotes

**Multiple Export Formats**
- Copy - Copy the entire transcript to your clipboard instantly
- Text (.txt) - Download as a plain text file
- Subtitle (.srt) - Download as an SRT file for video players
- Markdown (.md) - Download with the video title and link

**Clean, Non-Intrusive Design**
- Appears on the right side of YouTube, above recommendations
- Collapsible panel to save screen space
- Follows YouTube's dark/light theme automatically

**HOW TO USE**
1. Go to any YouTube video with captions
2. Find the "YouTube Transcript" panel on the right
3. Select your preferred language
4. Click timestamps to navigate or export the transcript

**PRIVACY FOCUSED**
- Transcript processing happens locally in your browser
- Anonymous usage analytics (Google Analytics 4) to improve the extension
- No personal data collection, no video content tracking
- No accounts required

Perfect for students, researchers, content creators, and anyone who wants to save or reference video content!
